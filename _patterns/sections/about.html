I'm a designer (musician, nutritionist) and photographer based in Ottawa, Canada. I specialize in obsessing over fine details while calmly translating information into pleasing visual experiences! Specifically, my areas of focus are branding, web and UX/UI design.

I like to be highly detail-oriented in each of these stages to produce meaningful work that speaks to each client and project's intended message.

Apart from design, I love reading, continuous learning, working on myself, spending time in nature, and, when it is safe to do so, making and playing music with the Haig.
